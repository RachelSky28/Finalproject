Day and Nightlife in NYC

By Rachel S. Khmelnitsky

Here is NYC sometimes you try to think of stuff to fill up time between meals and museums. So what to do inbetween in NYC? Here I have provided lists of prompts divided by day, night, and by season for some ideas for your visit! (or for the native and new New Yorkers, some ideas on what to do on your day off!)

I also have some of my favorite places I have spent my free time as a native New Yorker.

Key: For activities that are especially kid-friendly, I have included 👪, for 21+ activities I have included 🍷, and for activities with a cost I have included 💲

NYC Daylife

  • Walk around the parks
  • Tour some of the city’s architecture
  • Do a statue tour
  • Walk over one, or all, of our bridges
  • Find a pop up (I suggest not in the Winter if you see a line)
  • Grab afternoon tea 👪
  • Grab coffee or kava
  • Window shop, or just shop, at a flea or farmer’s market
  • Eat and buy trinkets at a seasonal food festival
  • Take the Staten Island ferry 👪
  • Visit Governor's Island
  • Visit a historic church
  • Visit a historic house (Revolutionary war history is strong here) 👪
  • Hit up a botanical garden or zoo 👪
  • Visit a library! 👪
  • Hit up an arcade 👪
  • Relax at a banya/bathhouse 👪
